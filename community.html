Godsent Kitchen Seafood Okra-A Must Try!

   

“Loaded with fresh seafood - this is the most authentic Seafood Okra pot I’ve had in all of Benin City. Rich palm extract, crunchy whole snails, fresh catfish. At 12,500 per pot it serves 4-6 people, so it’s actually great value.”

 
 
      Food Explorer    84 12    
 
 
       

Isi-Ewu Night at Tripple J

   

“The best Isi-Ewu in Aduwawa hands down. At 9,000 for a full pot, you get a generous serve perfect for sharing with friends and cold drinks. Very authentic and spicy - the goat head is medicinal and hot just right.”

   
 
      Benin Night Life  56 7        
 
 
       

YOLO Restaurant: Best Shawarma

   

“The loaded Shawarma at YOLO is genuinely packed with fillings. At 5,000 for the loaded version, it’s worth every naira. The Big Spicy Turkey (6500) is also exceptional and spicy with a nice kick. 9am-11pm means you can go late.”

 
   
      Ugbowo Foodie      73 19       
 
 
       

Greyfield Restaurant: Pizza in Benin City?

   

“Yes, you can get good pizza in Benin City. Greyfield on Airport Road does an Ultimate Cheese Lovers Medium at 10,500 and Chicken Pizza at 10,000. Also try the 3 Scoop Ice Cream (4500). Incredible variety for a local restaurant.”

 
   
      Airport Road Bites      91 24       
 
 
       

Sip and Dip Restaurant-Sapele Road

   

“The Sip and Dip Platter at 15,500 is a whole experience. The Native Rice with diced beef, kpomo and snails(6000) is also brilliant. 8am-9pm daily on Sapele Road opposite NPDC.”

Authentic Amala Spot-Old School Done Right

   

“The Amala, Ewedu and Gbegiri combo here is the most authentic I’ve found in Ugbowo. At 2,700 for the full combo with goat meat, it’s incredile value. Opens at 8am and closes at 6pm so go early - it sells out!.”
